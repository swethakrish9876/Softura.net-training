GULAB JAMUN

Ingredients

  1. One packet gulab jamun flour
  2. Two and half cup sugar
  3. Two cup water
  4. Cooking oil

Let's take a packet of gulab jamun flour add some water and mix it well, make sure the consistency should be same as how we make for chapathi .After getting proper consistency remain it for 10 minutes without disturbing it.

In the mean time make jeera ,to make jeera add two cup of water in separate vessel and boil it for 7 minutes after that add two and half cup of sugar into it ,mix it well and boil it for another 15 minutes.

Now , make small balls using the flour ,take another vessel add half litre of oil to fry those bowels ,after oil getting heated put the bowels into oil and fry it up untill getting golden colour.filter the oil replace the balls into jeera remain it for 1 hour .

Now your gulab jamun is ready to serve
